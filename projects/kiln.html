Late Ottoman period

The late Ottoman period is relatively speaking under-studied in Lebanon, but recent work is beginning to change this. This distribution shows the sites that have been recorded as late Ottoman in the EAMENA database.

Lime kiln
EAMENA-0205783
This 3D model of a lime kiln from the Jabal Moussa Biosphere Reserve, Mount Lebanon, was created during the 2019 Tahoun project. The structure likely dates to the late Ottoman period. Built into the steep natural slope, it made use of the region's natural and cultural resources to create lime.
